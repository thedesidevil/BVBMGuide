# ✅ BON VOYAGE BY MARINA  
## ALL-INCLUSIVE GUIDE QC CHECKLIST (FINAL SOP)

---

## 1️⃣ Mandatory Sections (Presence Check)

Ensure the guide includes ALL of the following:

- Cover Page (Title + Subtitle)  
- Table of Contents (clean, formatted)  
- Client Information + Trip Summary Page  
- Day-wise Itinerary (each day on a new page)  
- 🏥 Important Places Around Your Stay  
- 🎁 Souvenir Shopping Guide  
- 🍽️ Must-Try Local Dishes  
- 🚆 Getting Around (Transport Guide)  
- 🌍 Cultural Etiquette & Local Phrases  
- 🎒 Tailored Packing List  
- 📱 Mobile Connectivity Guide  
- 🚨 Safety & Emergency Contacts  
- 💉 Health & Vaccination Guidance  
- 🙏 Thank You Page  

---

## 2️⃣ Cover Page & Title Quality

- Title is creative, premium, and destination-relevant  
  - Example: *Sakura Adventures: Journey Through Japan*  
- Subtitle clearly summarizes trip  
- No generic naming (e.g., “Europe Trip Plan”)  
- Clean, uncluttered layout  

---

## 3️⃣ Table of Contents (TOC)

- TOC does NOT include:
  - Cover page  
  - TOC itself  
- All entries have consistent formatting  
- Emojis used consistently  
- Headings styled uniformly  
- Dotted lines connect section → page number  
- Page numbers match actual pages  

---

## 4️⃣ Client Information Page

- Client name correct  
- Number of pax + kids ages correct  
- Dietary preferences clearly mentioned  
- Departure city mentioned  
- Itinerary summary in tabular format:
  - City  
  - Dates  
  - Hotel (with Google Maps link)  
- Transport summary included  
- Information is clean and easy to scan  

---

## 5️⃣ Itinerary Accuracy (Day-wise Logic)

### A. Day Headers
- Format: *Day X: Day, Date – Title*  
- Day, date, and sequence correct  

---

### B. First Day (Arrival Flow)
- Complete journey from home → destination covered  
- All modes included (flight/train/taxi etc.)  
- Instructions clear and executable  

---

### C. Daily Flow Logic

For EACH day:

- Start time aligns with reality  
- No irrelevant suggestions  
- Activities follow logical order  
- No backtracking or zig-zag routes  
- Travel times realistic  
- Buffers included  
- Energy pacing balanced  
- Sunrise/sunset activities correctly placed  
- End of day clearly defined  

---

### D. Activity Accuracy

- Attractions open on that specific day  
- Timings accurate (including split hours)  
- Enough time before closing  
- Entry fees realistic  
- Booking requirements mentioned  
- Timed entries aligned with flow  

---

### E. Transport Within Day

- Mode is practical  
- Instructions clear and actionable  
- Matches overall transport strategy  

---

### F. Restaurants (Per Day)

- Minimum 3 options (where applicable)  
- Matches dietary preferences  
- Avoid irrelevant cuisine recommendations  
- Open at recommended time  
- Full opening hours mentioned  
- Located near:
  - Attractions (lunch)  
  - Hotel (dinner)  
- Distance reasonable  
- “(Insta Worthy)” used selectively  

---

### G. Google Maps Links (Global Rule)

Ensure ALL have links:

- Hotels  
- Attractions  
- Restaurants  
- Shopping places  
- Essential services  

---

### H. Real-World Practicality Check

- Day is executable without confusion  
- No unrealistic rushing  
- Clear tips included  
- Avoids common mistakes  

---

## 6️⃣ Important Places Around Your Stay

For EACH city/accommodation:

- Grocery / convenience store listed  
- Pharmacy listed (prefer longer hours)  
- Hospital listed:
  - 24x7  
  - Emergency capable  
- Google Maps links included  
- Opening hours mentioned  
- Directions provided  
- Distance reasonable  

---

## 7️⃣ Souvenir Shopping Guide

- Location-specific (NOT generic)  
- Items relevant to destination  
- Shops open at recommended times  
- Aligns with itinerary flow  
- Avoid low-quality tourist traps  
- Practical tips included  

---

## 8️⃣ Must-Try Local Dishes

- Matches dietary preferences  
- Authentic to destination  
- Veg alternatives included where needed  
- No impractical items  

---

## 9️⃣ Getting Around (Transport Guide)

- Matches transport summary  
- Ticketing method explained  
- Pass recommendations logical  
- Where/how to buy included  
- Apps/tools mentioned if useful  
- No unnecessary complexity  

---

## 🔟 Cultural Etiquette & Local Phrases

- Information accurate  
- Only relevant points included  
- Phrases correct and usable  

---

## 1️⃣1️⃣ Packing List

- Season-specific  
- Destination variations covered  
- Activity-specific callouts included  
- Religious/cultural requirements mentioned  
- Practical (not excessive)  

---

## 1️⃣2️⃣ Mobile Connectivity

- Accurate and up to date  
- Clear recommendation (SIM/eSIM/roaming)  
- Where to buy explained  
- Cost expectations realistic  

---

## 1️⃣3️⃣ Safety & Emergency

- Emergency numbers included  
- Common scams highlighted  
- Indian embassy details included  
- Practical, actionable advice  

---

## 1️⃣4️⃣ Health & Vaccination

- Accurate and relevant  
- Not excessive or alarmist  
- Clear distinction: required vs optional  

---

## 1️⃣5️⃣ Look & Feel / Premium Finish (CRITICAL)

- No spelling mistakes  
- No grammatical errors  
- No broken links  
- Consistent formatting  
- Clean spacing and alignment  
- Proper visual hierarchy  
- No clutter or inconsistencies  
- Feels premium, not templated  

---

## 1️⃣6️⃣ Final Mindset Check (Most Important)

Before sending, ask:

- If this were my own vacation, would I trust this completely?  
- Would I be able to execute this without stress?  
- Does this feel thoughtful, not generic?  
- Have we anticipated questions before the client asks?  

---

## ✨ Final Note

A guide is ready only when:

> There is nothing left that could confuse, mislead, or disappoint the client.
