# All Inclusive Guide (AIG) - Structure & Format Documentation

> This document captures the structure, format, and content patterns observed from the sample Peru AIG prepared by Bon Voyage by Marina.

## Overview

An **All Inclusive Guide (AIG)** is a comprehensive travel document prepared for clients based on their specific travel itineraries. It serves as a complete reference for:
- Day-by-day activities and logistics
- Restaurant and dining recommendations
- Practical travel information
- Cultural insights and tips
- Emergency contacts and health guidance

## Document Structure

Each page includes the company branding header:

```
Shop Act MH: 104297592503   Contact Number: +91-86000-15316
Udyam: UDYAM-MH-26-0889139  Email ID: hello@bonvoyagebymarina.com
```

### 2. Title & Table of Contents (Page 1)
- Document title: `All Inclusive Guide- [Destination]`
- Table of Contents with clickable links to all sections
- Uses emoji icons: 󰐟 for day entries, 🛒 🎁 🎎 🧳 🚨 📶 💉 for supplementary sections

### 3. Day-by-Day Itinerary Sections

Each day follows a consistent structure:

```
󰐟
Day [N]: [Date] — [Title/Summary]

[Time of Day Icon] [Activity Period] — [Activity Name]
● 📍 [Location/Departure details]
● ⏰ [Timing information]
● [Activity description]

🍽 [Meal] Recommendations in [Location]

[Restaurant Name]
● [Description of restaurant - atmosphere, specialty, reputation]
● 🍴 Must-Try: [Specific dish recommendations]
● ⏰ [Operating hours] | 💰 [Price range: ₹₹ or PEN XX-XX]
● 🚶 [Distance from hotel] OR 🚖 [Taxi time from hotel]

✅ Stay Overnight in [Location]
```

#### Time Period Icons Used:
- ✈ - Flights and transfers
- 🌄 - Morning/Evening activities
- 🧭 - Special notes/tips
- 🛳 - Boat/water activities
- 🍫 - Food/culinary activities
- 🌳 - Nature/outdoor activities
- 🏰 - City tours/sightseeing

### 4. Supplementary Sections

#### 󰐟 Must-Try Dishes Section
- National/regional specialty dishes
- Detailed descriptions of each dish
- **Vegetarian-Friendly** alternatives noted with ○ sub-bullets
- **Popular Drinks** subsection

#### 🛒 Important Places Around Your Stays
Lists essential nearby locations for each hotel:
- Grocery stores (with hours and distance)
- Pharmacies (with hours and distance)

#### 🎁 Souvenir Shopping Guide
- What to buy and where to buy it
- Quality tips and shopping advice
- General shopping tips (haggling, quality checks, payment tips)

#### 🎎 Cultural Etiquette & Useful Local Phrases
- **Dos** - things to do
- **Don'ts** - things to avoid
- **Essential Phrases** organized by category:
  - Greetings
  - Politeness
  - Requests & Questions
  - Dietary Needs
  - General

#### 🧳 Packing List
Organized by context:
- 👚 Essentials (general items)
- 🏔 Destination-specific gear (e.g., Sacred Valley, Machu Picchu)
- 🌳 Environment-specific gear (e.g., Amazon Rainforest)
- 🧴 Essential and Personal Items

#### 🚨 Personal Safety & Emergency Contacts
- Numbered safety tips with sub-bullets
- Emergency numbers with country codes
- Embassy contact information (for Indian travelers)

#### 📶 Mobile Connectivity & SIM Card Guide
- eSIM providers
- Local SIM card options

#### 💉 Health & Vaccination Guidance
- Recommended vaccinations
- Region-specific health requirements (e.g., yellow fever for Amazon)
- Medication notes
- Closing message: "Have an amazing time!"

## Formatting Conventions

### Emoji Usage
| Emoji | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| 󰐟 | Day section header |
| 📍 | Location/departure point |
| ⏰ | Time/duration information |
| 🚖 | Transfer/taxi |
| 🚶 | Walking distance |
| 🍽 | Meal/restaurant section |
| 🍴 | Must-try dishes |
| 💰 | Price indicator |
| ✅ | Stay overnight confirmation |
| 🧭 | Pro tips/special notes |

### Price Indicators
- `₹₹` - Moderate pricing
- `PEN XX-XX` - Specific price range in local currency (Peruvian Sol)
- `PEN 100+` - Higher-end pricing

### Distance/Time Indicators
- `X min walk from the hotel`
- `X min drive from the hotel`
- `X min taxi drive from the hotel`

### Bullet Point Hierarchy
- `●` - Main points
- `○` - Sub-points (especially for vegetarian alternatives)

## Content Characteristics

### Restaurant Recommendations
Each restaurant entry includes:
1. **Name** (hyperlinked to Google Maps)
2. **Description** - atmosphere, reputation, specialty
3. **Must-Try dishes** - specific menu recommendations
4. **Hours** - opening/closing times, closure days
5. **Price range** - relative or absolute pricing
6. **Distance** - walking or driving time from accommodation

> **Note**: All venue names (restaurants, hotels, attractions, pharmacies, grocery stores) are hyperlinked to Google Maps. The Peru sample uses multiple link formats:
> - **Google Knowledge Graph**: `https://g.co/kgs/XXXXX` (for hotels, well-known places)
> - **Google Maps Short**: `https://maps.app.goo.gl/XXXXX` (for restaurants)
> - **Google Maps Full**: `https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=VENUE+NAME` (rare)
>
> Total venue links in Peru sample: **45 links** across 34 pages

### Activity Descriptions
- Provide context and significance (historical, cultural)
- Include practical details (meeting times, what to bring)
- Note special experiences or highlights

### Personalization Elements
- Document title includes client names: `All Inclusive Guide - Peru- Neha and Mayur`
- Considers dietary preferences (vegetarian options highlighted)
- Tailored to specific travel dates
- Departure times calculated from their specific hotels
